📋 Attendance Guide

1

Allow Camera Access

When prompted, tap "Allow" to give camera permission. This is required to record attendance.

2

Align Your Face

Position your face inside the oval frame. Keep your face centered and well-lit.

3

Frame Turns Green → Tap Capture

Once the oval turns green, the camera button activates. Tap the blue camera icon to take your photo.

4

Allow Location

Location is checked to verify you're on school premises. You must be within the allowed area.

5

Check In & Check Out

First tap = Check In (morning). Come back at the end of the day and tap again for Check Out.
